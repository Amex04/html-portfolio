Project Overview

Eatrite is a food delivery app designed to connect users with local restaurants, providing a seamless ordering experience. The app allows users to browse menus, place orders, and track deliveries in real-time.

Eatrite

Experience ultimate convenience with Eatrite, the premier food delivery app that brings your favorite restaurant meals to your doorstep. Born out of a passion to revolutionize food delivery, our app offers a seamless and enjoyable experience, connecting you with top local eateries and delivering delicious meals with ease.

Development

Eatrite was born out of my UI/UX design project at Schoolville, where I honed my skills under the guidance of experienced instructors. From research to iteration, I poured my heart into creating a seamless food delivery experience. With valuable feedback and criticism, I refined my design to perfection. Today, Eatrite is a testament to my passion for user-centered design and innovation.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
  • Real-time order tracking
  • Secure payment options
  • Personalized recommendations based on user preferences
  • Integration with local restaurants for diverse food options

Design Process

The design process for Eatrite involved extensive user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. The goal was to create an intuitive and visually appealing interface that enhances the user experience.

Tools Used

  • Figma for UI/UX design
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end development

The Future of Eatrite

Eatrite is not just a food delivery app; it's a vision for the future of food delivery. As we continue to innovate, we aim to introduce features like AI-driven meal recommendations, enhanced user personalization, and expanded partnerships with local restaurants. Our goal is to create a platform that not only delivers food but also enriches the dining experience for our users.
